Introducing Shift&Code

Hi, I'm Gerianne. I'm a career Shifter who decided to dive into the world of Code. My educational background and work experience are in International Relations but somewhere along the way, I decided to do something else: I wanted to code. Since working in Tech is a new adventure for me,I wanted to share my experiences, the good and bad, and hopefully along the way, I could help others with their transition as a coder. Thus, Shift&Code was born.

If you're interested in shifting as well, then join me in this new chapter of my life and stay posted.
Maybe you can find something useful for you too.

Why did you shift into Tech?

To be honest, I didn't consider venturing into Coding right away. I thought of other industries I could work in because of the idea that, perhaps I just lost interest in my job. So I thought that if I transfer to a different work place or a different industry, then maybe I'll be happy with my job again. So I considered becoming a Paralegal, going to Law School, enrolling in Grad School, becoming a Writer, and I even considered working for any related positions at other companies. Well, out of all of them, I got into Grad School. About the same time I got accepted, I saw an ad for a Front End Web Dev Course. Now to be clear, I had an idea about coding because I coded back in Middle School and also through FreeCodeCamp.
