Introducing Shift&Code

Hi, I'm Gerianne. I'm a career Shifter who decided to dive into the world of Code. My educational background and work experience are in International Relations but somewhere along the way, I decided to do something else: I wanted to code. Since working in Tech is a new adventure for me, I wanted to share my experiences and hopefully along the way, I could help others with their transition as a coder. Thus, Shift&Code was born.

If you are interested in shifting as well, or you are just curious with what it's like, then join me in this new chapter of my life and stay posted.
Maybe you can find something useful for you too.
